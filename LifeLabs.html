Main Problem and Root Cause:

Animal feeds spoil quickly, causing wastage and higher costs of maintenance for farmers. Other problem we found is that poor storage conditions like heat, moisture, and air exposure, which lead to mold and bacteria growth. As a result, feeds lose quality and must be discarded for safety reasons, increasing expenses and reducing productivity. That leads us to our Solution with a physical Protoype.

Solution and Protoype:

The solution we came up with comes from the objective. We pursue to develop a sensored feeding machine that is tight and secure. It aims to prevent rodents and other pests from getting in and ensuring to feed animals the right amount of animal feed.

Furthermore, our solution uses a sensored feeding machine with thight, secure storage to ensure rodents and other pests cannot get in. This machine also gives the right amount of animal feed to be given to our animals in the right time. In fact this also keeps the feeds fresh and protected from spoilage.

Conclusion

Our prototype is for animals to have the right portions of animal feed, not only that it also prevents rodents, and pests from getting in the feed. Our prototype also aims to make the feed last much longer by preventing it from spoiling quickly. The prototype is the perfect thing for animals for keeping their feeds clean and fresh with no contamination included. In addition to that it keeps the feed organized and clean looking.
